What is this?

VirusClicker is a time-wasting clicking game inspired by the infamous CookieClicker. The goal of the game is to infect as many people as possible, while also upgrading your virus to epic new levels.

Why make something so darn useless?

To mess around with Angular.js. Check out the source code: VirusClicker.
